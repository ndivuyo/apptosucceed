Internships and Volunteer Positions

(Virtual or Stamford, CT)

Are you a college student who wants to strengthen your resume/portfolio or a professional who would like to give some time and expertise to a worthy cause?

As a nonprofit we cannot offer payment, but we can:

  • tailor the internship to your interests, abilities and availability
  • provide an enriching experience, where your contribution will make a difference
  • acknowledge your name on our website
  • award a letter of recommendation from the President of App to Succeed, Inc. for exceptional contributions.

Eligibility:

  • Professionals that would like to volunteer for a good cause.
  • College students with related experience and course work or major looking to strengthen their resumes and portfolios.
  • *Local high school students eligible for positions as designated below.

Hours and dates are flexible, but we request a minimum of 15 hours per week for short term (four-month) internships.
