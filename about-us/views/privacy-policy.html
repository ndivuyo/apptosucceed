What personally identifiable information is collected and how is it used?

Contact Information
When you register on www.ApptoSucceed.org you voluntarily provide us with contact information (such as name and e-mail address). We may use this information for specific, limited purposes, i.e. to contact you with information about App to Succeed and its programs. You may “opt out,” at any time, now or in the future, if you do not wish to receive our messages by emailing MoneyMaze@apptosucceed.org. Please enter “Opt Out” in subject line.

Private Profile Information
Map the Money Maze subscribers will be asked to provide personal information, (e.g. grade point average, household income) for purposes of providing the most personalized and effective educational-learning experience possible. To subscribe or donate, an established transaction service will process your transaction. This service will have agreed not share your credit card or account information unless you give your explicit permission.

IP addresses
App to Succeed uses your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, to administer www.ApptoSucceed.org, and for statistical metrics used to track how visitors use our website and how subscribers use Map the Money Maze.

Cookies
www.ApptoSucceed.org uses “cookie” messages to automatically help provide better services. They remind us who you are and your preferences for our website based on information you have previously provided. The “cookie” is placed in your computer and is read when you come back to our website. Cookies let us take you to the information and features you’re particularly interested in. They also let us track your usage of www.ApptoSucceed.org, so we can continue to improve our programs. You can reject cookies or cancel them by instructing your Web browser accordingly. Map the Money Maze is highly interactive software and must use cookies to give instantaneous feedback to each subscriber and to customize the program to the subscriber’s personal profile and previous answers.

Email Privacy

App to Succeed does not provide, sell, or rent email addresses to anyone outside the organization.
