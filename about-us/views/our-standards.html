App to Succeed aims to:

  • Be a trusted partner.
  • Provide effective personal finance education to Americans who need it the most.
  • Provide credible content, (e.g. from impartial sources that have no commercial intent.)
  • Offer content that is comprehensive, relevant to the learner, and has the potential to have a positive effect on the participant’s short and long term financial well-being.
  • Conduct the organization’s operations and activities with integrity.
  • Protect the privacy of all personal information (unless the participant gives their explicit consent to share it). See Privacy Policy

If for any reason, your experience with App to Succeed does not meet these standards, kindly email us at MoneyMaze@apptosucceed.org so that we can make appropriate adjustments. Thank you.
